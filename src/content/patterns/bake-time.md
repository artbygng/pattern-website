## What's inside

Bake Time is a 8-page crochet quiet book centered around a baking theme. The different pages have interactive pieces for children to play with, including a memory game and a page for learning about counting.
The bright colors and many different options for playing make this a great gift which can last as a wonderful childhood memory.</br>
There are removable pieces, so adult supervision is recommended for younger children.

### The pages
**The cover** — The cover page depicts a lovely, colorful birthday cake with dripping frosting. The book can be fastened off with a velcro strap.


**Piece of cake!** — A scalloped pie sits center-page. Allowing fine motor skills practice and imaginative play by placing and removing the toppings: a strawberry, a dollop of whipped cream, a lemon slice, and a small cherry. Everything velcros on and lifts right off. 

**Stand Mixer** — The red stand mixer has a bowl and a moving whisk. Three baskets hold three eggs which can be placed in the bowl and whisked. The start to every good cake!

**Cupcake Shop** — Each cupcake has its own color-coordinated liner. Mix and match the colors or place them all in their place. Great for learning about colors!

**Chocolate Chip Cookie** — A big cookie and chocolate chips which can be pulled in different directions. Next to it: a milk bottle in its own little pocket. Simple, satisfying.

**5-tier Cake** — Five numbered cake tiers in descending size. Discover the colorful circles beneath the numbers. Counting, colors, and fine motor in one.

**Cookie Dough** — Roll the dough with the roller included in the instructions and create different shapes of cookies. Each cookie attaches with a velcro and has its location marked with a dotted line. Great for practicing shapes.

**Memory Game Cookies** — A memory game dressed up as cookies! Each cookie can be flipped over to reveal a different shape and color. Memorize their locations to match all 6 pairs.
