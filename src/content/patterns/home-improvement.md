## What's inside the pattern

Home Improvement is a 6-page crochet quiet book built around a home repair theme. Each page is a different room or task — a whole little house to fix up, one job at a time. There are real tools to pick up and put down, doors that open and close, pictures to hang on the wall, pipes to unclog, windows to scrub, and a bathtub to seal.
Bold colors, satisfying activities, and a full toolkit — a great gift for toddlers who love to "help" with things and feel like they're really doing something.</br>
There are removable pieces, so adult supervision is recommended for younger children.

### The pages
**The House — Welcome!** — A grey house facade with a crocheted red tile roof. The two cream windows velcro open and shut with tiny loop latches. The dark blue door swings on crocheted hinges — open it to see the heart embroidered inside, close it and hook the pin. Loose roof tiles can be placed and rearranged across the roof.

**Painting the Wall** — An onion-skin wall with three colored paint blocks (red, dark blue, yellow) hidden under question-mark flap covers. Lift the flap to see what's underneath. A 3D paint brush sits ready in its holder on the side of the page.

**Hanging a Picture** — A lilac wall with three different picture frames — a sun and umbrella scene, a star, and a flower — all hanging from a crocheted net. Take a frame down, swap them around, and put them back using the 3D hammer and a tiny crocheted nail.

**Unclogging the Sink** — A teal sink with a crocheted faucet and handle, plumbing underneath (vertical, horizontal, and curved tubes), and a yellow wrench in a holder. The curved pipe detaches with velcro so little hands can "fix" the clog and put it back.

**Cleaning the Window** — A window with grey dirt blobs that velcro on and off. The spray cleaning bottle (with nozzle and handle) and a yellow crocheted rag each sit in their own net pockets. Spray, scrub, and peel the dirt away.

**Sealing the Bathtub** — A light turquoise bathtub with a green shower curtain on a rod, red and blue taps, a shower head with pipes, and a crocheted silicone sealant tube in its own net pocket. A full bathroom scene — run the shower, open the curtain, seal the edge.
