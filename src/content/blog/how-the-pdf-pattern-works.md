For buyers who've never bought a digital crochet pattern before and aren't sure what they're actually getting.

## Outline
- What "PDF instant download" means in practice: where it comes from (Etsy), how it's delivered, no physical item ships
- Structure of a typical pattern PDF: materials list, per-page written instructions, step photos, assembly instructions at the end
- Terminology note: patterns are written in US crochet terms
- What's not included (no video, no kit/yarn) and why that's stated upfront
- CTA: link to a sample pattern page (skill notes section) as a preview
