**This post is a repeatable template, not a one-off.** Duplicate this file per customer (e.g. `customer-spotlight-danielle-fun-foods.md`), swap in their name, pattern, and photos, and set `draft: false` with a real `pubDate` when ready to publish.

## Outline for each spotlight
- One or two customer photos of their finished book (with permission)
- A short quote from the customer about making it (Etsy reviews are a good source, with permission to reuse)
- What pattern they made and anything they customized (colors, theme swaps, page choices)
- A line or two on why their version stood out
- CTA: link to the pattern they made, plus an invite for other makers to share their own finished books for a future spotlight
