Directly addresses the fear of buying a pattern and getting stranded partway through it — a real purchase blocker for PDF-only patterns with no video.

## Outline
- Reassure up front: getting stuck is normal and doesn't mean the pattern (or the maker) failed
- What's included to prevent getting stuck: step-by-step photos, written instructions, stitch counts at each stage
- How to actually get help if stuck: contact channel (email/Instagram/Etsy message), what to include when asking (page name, step number, a photo)
- Response-time expectation, kept honest and modest
- CTA: link to the contact section on the About page
