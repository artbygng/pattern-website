Answers a practical planning question, especially for buyers making a book as a gift with a deadline (birthday, holiday).

## Outline
- Set expectations: total hours vary widely by page count and experience level — give a rough range, not a false precise number
- Break it down per page vs. per book, and call out assembly time separately
- Realistic pacing for "an hour or two a few evenings a week" vs. a dedicated crafting weekend
- Planning-backward tip: if you need it by a date, work back from assembly + buffer time
- CTA: link to a specific pattern's stated skill level/page count as a worked example
